# Conversion Rate Optimisation (CRO)

CRO for marketing and product pages:
- Funnel analysis and drop-off identification
- A/B test design and hypothesis generation
- Form and checkout flow optimisation
- Landing page layout and CTA placement
- Heatmap and session recording analysis
- Data-driven UX improvements
